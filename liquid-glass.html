Apple's new Liquid Glass

Apple has just announced Liquid Glass, a revolutionary update to the iOS interface. This new design features transparency to create a 'Glass Feel,' complemented by smooth transitions and pixel-perfect animations.
It's the most significant design change since iOS 7. The 'Fluid Like Design' ensures a cleaner and more intuitive iOS experience. Additionally, navbars now shrink as you scroll down, enhancing navigation efficiency.
Menu items are now displayed vertically instead of horizontally, making them easier and more accessible for users.
