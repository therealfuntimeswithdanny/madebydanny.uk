iOS 26 iMessage Backgrounds, Additive Clock & Games App first hand.

iOS 26 is the biggest update to design since iOS 7, Apple announced iMessage Backgrounds, Additive Clocks and a New Games App.



iMessage Backgrounds

iMessage backgrounds let you add personality to your messages, making every chat feel fresh and unique. It also adds contrast to your messages, making the text pop or creating a visually engaging gradient.

With customization options, you can choose backgrounds that match your mood. From vibrant colors to subtle textures, the choices are tailored to give your messages a personal or professional touch.



Additive Clocks

I love Additive clocks for their seamless ability to adjust time with height updates, showcasing Apple’s signature smooth design.

The animations and customizable layouts that has an awesome style. The video below (Comming Soon) provides a closer look at how these features enhance the user experience like never before.



Games App

It’s like Steam for iPhone, with all your recently played games, achievements, and trending titles conveniently organized in one place. You can play with friends, track your progress, and discover new favorites, all while enjoying seamless controller support.

Essentially, it brings the full gaming ecosystem right to your phone, redefining mobile gaming in an exciting way.
